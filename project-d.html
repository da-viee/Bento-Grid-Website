Packaging Design Render

Photorealistic packaging render with full environment setup, material studies, and print-ready output files for a retail product.

ClientConfidential
Category3D Render
ToolsBlender, Substance Painter, Photoshop
DeliverablesMaterial Studies, Environment Render, Print Files

The Brief

A retail brand needed photorealistic packaging renders for their e-commerce listings and ad campaigns. The renders had to be indistinguishable from photography while offering the flexibility of CG — unlimited angles, lighting adjustments, and zero product photography costs.

Our Approach

We modelled the packaging from scratch in Blender, created multiple material variants (matte, gloss, foil), and produced renders from 6 camera angles in a hero studio environment. Final outputs were delivered as 6K TIFF files.
