Motion Graphics

Brand Animation

Bespoke brand motion identity — logo reveal, animated transitions, lower-thirds, and a full motion style guide for a digital-first campaign.

ClientConfidential
CategoryMotion Graphics
ToolsAfter Effects, Cinema 4D, Illustrator
DeliverablesLogo Reveal, Transitions, Lower-thirds, Motion Guide

The Brief

The client required a complete motion identity system to accompany their rebrand. Every touchpoint — from social media posts to YouTube intros — needed to feel cohesive, kinetic, and distinctly premium.

Our Approach

We designed a modular motion system: a core logo reveal animation (3 seconds), a library of 12 transition wipes, animated lower-thirds for video production, and a comprehensive motion style guide document for their internal team.
