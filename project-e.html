Motion Graphics

Visual Effects Reel

VFX compositing for a premium product ad — particle systems, light leaks, chromatic aberrations, and cinematic colour grading from grade-to-grade.

ClientConfidential
CategoryMotion Graphics / VFX
ToolsAfter Effects, DaVinci Resolve, Nuke
DeliverablesVFX Composites, Colour Grade, Master Export

The Brief

A premium lifestyle brand needed cinematic VFX layered onto their existing product footage — to transform standard camera footage into a high-end commercial feel without a full reshoot budget.

Our Approach

We motion-tracked the original footage, composited particle systems and volumetric light effects frame-by-frame in After Effects, then applied a custom cinema colour grade in DaVinci Resolve to achieve the luxury aesthetic the brand required.
