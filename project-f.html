Digital Campaign

Multi-platform animated ad set across TikTok, YouTube pre-roll, and display banners — designed for a lifestyle brand's seasonal campaign.

ClientConfidential
CategorySocial Ads
ToolsAfter Effects, Premiere Pro, Figma
DeliverablesTikTok Ads, YouTube Pre-roll, Display Banners (6 sizes)

The Brief

A lifestyle brand launching their new seasonal range needed animated ads across every digital platform simultaneously — TikTok, YouTube, and a full suite of display banner sizes for Google Display Network.

Our Approach

We designed a single master animation then adapted it across all formats — starting with the TikTok vertical (9:16), cutting a 15-second YouTube pre-roll, and building a full HTML5 banner set in 6 standard IAB sizes. All assets were delivered within a 2-week timeline.
