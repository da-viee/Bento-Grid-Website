3D Render

Product Visualisation

A high-end 360° product render created for a consumer brand launch campaign — featuring clay renders, lit stills, and animated turntable.

Client Confidential
Category 3D Render
Tools Blender, Cinema 4D, After Effects
Deliverables Still renders, Turntable video, Styleframes

The Brief

The client needed a powerful, premium 3D product animation to anchor their new flagship launch. The goal was to communicate premium materials and innovative form through motion, while keeping the visual language clean and modern for a social media-first rollout.

Our Approach

We built the full product in Blender, focusing on accurate material simulation — anisotropic metals, soft-touch plastics, and glass. The camera moves were choreographed to tell a story of reveal: starting with the clay form, then introducing light and texture, and finishing with a live-surface animated turntable.
